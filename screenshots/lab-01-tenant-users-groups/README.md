## Sanitization Requirements

Before uploading screenshots publicly, remove or blur:

- Tenant IDs
- Subscription IDs
- Real email addresses
- Personal Microsoft account information
- Phone numbers
- Recovery emails
- MFA methods
- QR codes
- Temporary passwords
- Billing information
- Secrets, tokens, or keys

## Notes

Only fake lab identities should be visible in screenshots.
